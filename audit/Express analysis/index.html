Express analysis

Along with the common and detailed types of audit, we bring to your attention another, very attractive service-express (express analysis, audit of tax risks), not involving a thoroughly verification of individual business transactions.

Express analysis - assessment of the state of accounting and tax accounting of the organization for the indicated period with the recommendations of specialists. The use of the express analysis service is advisable if the full audit is not relevant for you.

Express Audit is most effective before preparing the annual balance and tax audit. You can quickly receive reliable information about the real state of affairs at the enterprise in a short time and make managerial decisions, timely reducing tax risks and increasing the efficiency of the organization.

Express analysis is recommended in the following cases:

  • When replacing the chief accountant
  • When changing the owner of the organization
  • When evaluating tax risks for the periods preceding the tax audit
  • during reorganization and restructuring of the enterprise
  • upon receipt of the loan and in other cases

 

Express Audit is carried out in a short time — up to five working days.As a result of its implementation, you will receive an audit report containing a list of shortcomings found, recommendations for their elimination and optimization of the organization. This document is compiled exclusively for internal use (for managers and employees of the internal units of the enterprise).

The basic cost of express audit is from 1000 Dirham. for checking one calendar year.

 

Advantages of the audit with us:

  • The extensive experience of our auditors (more than 10 years in the field of audit and accounting service)
  • Minimization of tax risks
  • strict observance of confidentiality
  • The client’s interests are always in the first place
  • Good faith and hard work
